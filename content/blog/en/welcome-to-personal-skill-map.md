We're excited to introduce Personal Skill Map, an innovative platform designed to help professionals visualize and accelerate their career growth.

## What is Personal Skill Map?

Personal Skill Map is an AI-powered web application that transforms how you think about career development. Whether you're just starting out or looking to pivot into a new field, our platform helps you:

- **Visualize your skills** as an interactive, connected graph
- **Explore any career path** and see exactly what skills you need
- **Import your experience** from resumes, LinkedIn, or portfolios
- **Generate tailored resumes** optimized for specific job postings

## How It Works

### 1. Import Your Skills

Upload your resume in PDF, Word, or image format. Our AI extracts your skills, work experience, education, and projects automatically. You can also paste your LinkedIn URL or describe your background.

### 2. Explore Career Paths

Search for any career—from "Software Engineer" to "Product Manager" to "Data Scientist"—and see a comprehensive skill map showing the progression from beginner to advanced skills.

### 3. Track Your Progress

Mark skills as learned, in progress, or planned. Watch your skill map evolve as you grow professionally.

### 4. Generate Resumes

When you're ready to apply for jobs, paste a job posting URL and our AI generates a tailored resume highlighting your most relevant skills and experiences.

## Multi-Language Support

Personal Skill Map supports English, Chinese, and Japanese. Generate resumes in your preferred language regardless of your input language.
